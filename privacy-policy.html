Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026. This policy outlines how Data Parse handles sensitive information across our infrastructure.

1. Data Collection

We collect information necessary for the operation of our data processing services, including technical telemetry and account identifiers. All collection is performed under explicit consent or contractual necessity.

2. Processing & Storage

All client data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit via TLS 1.3. Information is processed within isolated secure clusters to ensure zero leakage between client environments.

3. Third-Party Integration

We do not sell data to third parties. We may utilize sub-processors for cloud infrastructure (e.g., AWS, GCP) under strict DPA (Data Processing Agreements).

4. Your Rights

Clients maintain the right to audit, export, or delete their processed data at any time through our Management Console or by contacting our Data Protection Officer.
