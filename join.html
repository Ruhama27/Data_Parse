Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need coding experience to join?

expand_more

Not at all. We have members at all levels, from complete beginners to advanced coders. We start every session from the basics and build up. The only requirement is curiosity.

How many sessions per week are required?

expand_more

There's no strict attendance requirement, though we encourage you to come to at least one session per week. The more you show up, the faster you'll grow. Sessions are Mon, Wed, and Sat.

What do I need to bring?

expand_more

A laptop with Python installed (we help set this up at the first session) and a willingness to learn. We provide all learning materials, datasets, and project resources for free.

Can I join mid-semester?

expand_more

Yes! Enrollment is always open. We'll pair you with a project team and get you caught up quickly. Our sessions are designed to be drop-in friendly.

Is there a leadership/officer role I can apply for?

expand_more

Yes! After a semester of active membership, you can apply for officer roles: VP of Projects, VP of Learning, Events Lead, and more. These look great on a CV.
